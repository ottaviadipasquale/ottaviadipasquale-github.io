Hi, I’m
Ottavia Dipasquale

Post-doctoral research associate at King's College London

After receiving my PhD in Bioengineering at Politecnico di Milano in 2016, I joined the Department of Neuroimaging at the IoPPN, King’s College London.
My research aims at developing novel techniques to combine the information about the neurochemical mechanisms that occur in the molecular substrate of the brain with its haemodynamic response measured with resting state fMRI.
